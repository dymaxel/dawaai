Odoo 12 Sales Combo (Enterprise)

This module is useful to sale the combo product.

Note: This module is tested and working in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

How to use

  • In Sales > Configuration, under the Combo Feature, Enable the Combo option.
  • In Product form, enable 'Is Combo' option to sale the Combo Product.


  • In Products, under the Combo tab, select the given option like required or optional, categories, products and number of item that you want to sale.


  • In Sales > Orders > Quotations , Create one order and select the product that you configured as combo.


  • In Sale Order, Click on the combo button to get the advantage of combo feature.
  • By Clicking on the Combo button, One popup will be display, now selected the optional product that you want to buy.
  • In Product form, When Select Default is enable in that case when you click on Combo button, popup will open with selected product(Selected Product is based on the No. of Items you have configure in product form).
  • By Clicking on the confirm, button picking of selected product is created.
  • Picking state of the childe product must be done if you want to confirm the SO.


  • In Sale Order, You can see the delivery of that products.


  • Here You can print the combo product report.
